  • How to Play
  • Watch how to play: click me to watch
  • Start the Game: Press the "Start" button to begin the game. Simon will display a random sequence of lights and sounds
  • Replicate the Sequence: Watch and listen carefully to the sequence of lights and sounds played by Simon. Your task is to repeat the exact sequence in the correct order.
  • Use the Buttons: Simon has four colored buttons - typically red, blue, green, and yellow. Each button corresponds to a different sound and lights up when activated.
  • Follow the Progression: As you correctly replicate each sequence, Simon will add another step, making the sequence longer. Pay close attention and remember the order of each step.
  • Continue and Score: Keep replicating the sequences correctly to increase your score. The game will continue until you make a mistake and press the wrong button or press them in the wrong order. Your goal is to achieve the highest score possible by accurately remembering and replicating the increasingly longer sequences.
